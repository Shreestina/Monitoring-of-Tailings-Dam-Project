MONITORING OF TAILINGS DAMS WITH PEIZOMETER READINGS

Peizometer is an instrument used for measuring underground water pressure. Thus, helps in monitoring of dams and embankments.(no.8 in the figure represents Peizometer).

This is a real case study of a data visualization of instrumentation on a Tailings Dam. The data represents pore water pressure readings caught up by Vibrating Wire Piezometers (VWP) in an existing embankment. The readings were collected from Windhoek in Africa.Here,readings from five different peizometers have been collected to see the trend of water pressure(kPA) from 2016 to 2021.
